Motion Design

During the pandemic, the isolation gave many of us the opportunity to explore new hobbies and interests. For me, it was a chance to dive into motion design using software like After Effects. Although I initially started creating animations featuring my favorite artists, this hobby quickly evolved into a deeper understanding of the design process and how to create visually appealing content.

I've been sharing my animations on Instagram, and I'm excited to share some of my current projects with you below. You can also follow my Instagram account for more of my work!
